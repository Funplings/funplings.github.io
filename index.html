Ni howdy! I'm Matthew Guo. I am currently based in NYC. I graduated from UC Berkeley with a B.A. in Computer Science in 2022 and am currently working as a backend software engineer, but at heart I'm an artist, and like to engage in various creative pursuits in my free time. I've been drawing/painting since I could hold a pencil, but in recent years I've also explored other creative fields, such as writing and filmmaking. This website is a portfolio of everything I've created that I'm proud of. I love connecting with people, so please feel free to reach out to me for any reason, whether you want to collaborate with me on a project, commission me for work, or even if you just think I'm cool and want to be friends!

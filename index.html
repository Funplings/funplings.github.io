Ni howdy! My name's Matthew Guo, but you may also know me by my online handles: funplings or glasshalftrue. I graduated from UC Berkeley with a B.A. in Computer Science in 2022 and am currently working as a backend software engineer based in NYC. But at heart I'm an artist, and I like to engage in various creative pursuits in my free time. I've been drawing/painting since I could hold a pencil, but in recent years I've also explored other creative avenues as well, such as writing and filmmaking. This website is a portfolio of everything I've created and shared online that I'm proud of. I love connecting with people, so please feel free to reach out to me for any reason, whether you want to collaborate with me on a project, commission me for work, or even if you just think I'm cool and want to be friends!
